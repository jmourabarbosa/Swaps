Overall Instructions

Do not feel demotivated if it gets too difficult as the number items increase, it's suposed to be difficult. Nevertheless, remember that you get more money when answering correctly the more difficult trials.

Short summary

You will be briefly shown a set of colored circles at random locations. At the end of each trial, you will be ask to report the color of one of the circles. You have to be very focused if you want to get the maximum reward possible ($5). Sporadically, the black box on the center of the screen will turn red and you have a fraction of a second to click it back to black. The fastest you do that, the higher your final reward will be multiplied . Each time you click a black box, your reward will decrease until it reaches the minimum ($0.50). If you never detect a red box or you never click it back to black, your reward will be minimum ($0.50). This means that the best strategy is to focus on the black box and avoid loking directly to the circles. This will ensure you will attend to all the circles equally and detect when the box changes color faster.

More detailed instrutions will follow. On the following screens, you will see a simulation of the kind of trials you can expect to encounter. Each page corresponds to a step on the real HIT but bare in mind that in the real HIT they will hapend one of after the other with no pause except at the end of each trial. Further in the Instructions you will see the formula we use to compute the final reward.
