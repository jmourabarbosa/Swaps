TITLE OF RESEARCH: Limits of working memory capacity
INVESTIGATOR: Joao Barbosa, Timothy Buschman, Albert Compte

The following informed consent is required by Princeton University for any person involved in a research study conducted by investigators at the University. This study has been approved by the University's Institutional Review Board for Human Subjects.

You will be asked to perform a task which will probe your working memory capacity limits. It's been reported in scientific literature that working memory capacity is correlated with IQ.

You will be briefly shown a set of colored circles on random locations. At the end of each trial, you will be ask to report the color of one of the circles. You have to be very focused if you want to get the maximum reward possible ($5). Sporadically, the black box on the center of the screen will turn red and you have a fraction of a second to click it back to black. The fastest you do that, the higher your final reward will be multiplied .
